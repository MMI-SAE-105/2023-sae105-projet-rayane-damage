Miles Morales'origins

Posted the : 18/10/2023

reading time : 5 min

We are a lot to have discovered Miles Morales in theaters, through the animated movies. But he is a character which origins were set-up back in the years of 2010.

The 4th issue of Ultimate Comics is a true treasure for any modern comics fan. Released in 2011, this issue is the work of the collaboration of two skilled creators of the comics industry: Brian Michael Bendis for the scenario and Sara Pitchelli for the illustrations. This team knew how to captivate the readers with a story taking place in an alternative universe of Marvel: The Ultimate universe. It was introduced by Marvel in the 2000s with the goal of modernizing their classical super-heroes, including Spiderman. This alternative side was allowing comic-book authors to explore new perspectives, to rethink the origins of super-heroes and to create an environment that is more anchored in the twenty-first century. This 4rth issue follows this spirit and proposes an alternative version of Spiderman: Miles Morales.

Miles'story

At the core of this story lies the now famous character of Miles Morales, a teenager that was bitten by a radioactive spider from another dimension. The duality between being an ordinary teen and being a super-hero is at the center of this adventure, just like with Peter Parker in 1963. But what makes this 4th issue of Ultimate Comics so important is the reception it has from the external medias and the public. Actually, the introducing of this character was praised for the diversity he adds to the Marvel universe, and he became a model for a lot of readers from minorities, proving that anyone could be a super-hero.

A cross-media character

Since his first apparition, Miles knew a phenomenal success and then collided with other medias. He now appears in his own comics series: Spiderman: Miles Morales, where he continues his quest as a modern Spiderman. But Miles is also present in the video game landscape with Marvel's Spiderman: Miles Morales, a videogame praised for its playablility and its qualitative narration. But the popularity of Miles reached its climax since the animated movie Spiderman: into the spider-verse which was a huge success and even received the Oscar of the best animated movie in 2019. It explores not only the adventures of Miles, but also of other Spider-heroes from different universes (forming the Spider-verse). That the sequel Across the spider-verse is reusing with even more depth and detail.

A long reach cultural phenomenon

We can conclude that the fourth issue of Ultimate Comics gave birth to a cultural phenomenon through Miles Morales, who weaved his web through diverse medias. This unique adventure is a must-have for any comic-book fan mindful of discovering the origins of a super-hero that embodies diversity and inspiration.
