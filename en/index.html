“Being Spiderman is a sacrifice”

Introduction

Spiderman: Across the spider-verse is a movie that is praised by its fans for the abundance and accuracy of the visual, sound and scenaristic details that it holds. This website mainly focuses on an important quote from the movie: “being Spiderman is a sacrifice”. It is analysed in depth in a dedicated article that explains its signification and how it can be interpreted. The website also contains articles on the film and other elements such as film directors, the origins of Miles Morales and so on!
