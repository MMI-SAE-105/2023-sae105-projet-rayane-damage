glossary

Spider-heros :

A spider-hero is a person that inherited spider-like superpowers.

Spider-verse :

The spider-verse (contraction of “spider” and “universe”) is a term used to describe all the universes where a spider-hero lives.

Frames :

n printing, a frame is a surface composed of lines or dots with predetermined spacing to obtain different tones of a same color.

Smearing :

In 2D animation, smearing traduces the quick movement of an object by representing its trajectory. It is similar to the concept of lines of movement.

Production designer :

In the cinema industry, a production designer is in charge of researching, planning and creating the backgrounds of a movie.

Storyboard :

A storyboard is a set of sketches representing the different keyframes of a movie. It’s used to visualise the framing without the need of making the complete scene.
