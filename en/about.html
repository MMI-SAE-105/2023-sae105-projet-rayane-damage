About

Hello, my name's Rayane Damage and I'm a student in the BUT MMI at Montbéliard in France. This website is my first semester project and the theme was “movie quote” and I chose “Being Spiderman is a sacrifice” from the movie Spiderman: Across the spider-verse. I chose it because I really love the unique visual aspect of the movie and I knew that I had things to talk about on it. Moreover, I found the quote very interesting to analyse. I tried to give the site a vibe that would remind the movie's moment when Miles learns about the canon events and when Miguel says his line.
